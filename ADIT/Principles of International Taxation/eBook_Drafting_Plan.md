# Exam Focus eBook Drafting Plan
## ADIT Module 1: Principles of International Taxation

---

## Executive Summary

This eBook drafting plan restructures the ADIT Module 1 syllabus for **effective exam-focused learning**, based on:
- Analysis of **8 past exam papers (2022-2025)**
- **Exam Focus methodology** principles
- **ADIT 2026 Syllabus** weightings and Level requirements

The restructured approach prioritizes topics by **exam frequency and strategic value**, teaches concepts through **actual past exam questions**, and builds skills progressively toward exam readiness.

## PART 2: RESTRUCTURED SYLLABUS FOR EFFECTIVE LEARNING

### Learning Sequence Rationale

The syllabus is restructured based on:
1. **Prerequisite relationships** - foundational concepts taught first
2. **Exam frequency** - high-frequency topics given more depth and examples
3. **Progressive complexity** - building from core concepts to integration
4. **Part A vs Part B preparation** - theory and scenario skills developed in parallel
